Parrot

Parrots are vibrant, colorful birds found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions around the world, including South and Central America, Africa, and Oceania. Known for their intelligence and ability to mimic human speech, parrots are social creatures that often live in large flocks. They have strong, curved beaks and zygodactyl feet, which aid them in climbing and feeding on seeds, nuts, fruits, and sometimes even insects. The family includes over 400 species, with some being popular pets due to their engaging personalities and remarkable vocal abilities.
